// frequently asked questions

How does matching work?

You choose up to 5 interests during profile setup. When you tap "next", we find someone online who shares your interests. You see their profile — name, interests, rating — and decide whether to connect or skip.

How do I report someone?

Type /report during a conversation, or tap the report button on the other user's profile card. Choose a reason and submit. Reports are reviewed and acted upon within 24 hours. You can submit up to 5 reports per day.

How do I block a user?

Type /block during a conversation. Blocked users will never be matched with you again. You can manage your block list from the dashboard settings.

How do I delete my account?

Open the dashboard, go to Settings, and tap "Delete Account" at the bottom. This permanently removes your profile, score, credits, and all associated data. This action cannot be undone.

What is the ranking system?

After each conversation, both users receive a grade based on conversation quality and duration. Quality conversations earn more points. Your rank (from NEW to LEGEND) reflects your overall standing and affects who you're matched with.

Is my data private?

Yes. We don't store chat messages after your session ends. We don't collect your name, email, phone number, or location. Your device is identified by a random ID stored only on your device. Read our full privacy policy for details.

How do I appeal a ban?

Open the app, go to the dashboard, tap Settings, and select "appeal a ban" under Support. Describe your situation — your device ID is attached automatically. We review all appeals and respond within 48 hours. You can submit one appeal at a time; if it is dismissed, you may resubmit after 7 days.

What are credits?

Credits are the in-app currency used to purchase themes and broadcast shouts. You earn credits passively through regular use, or purchase them via the in-app store.
