v1.2.0 Apr 28, 2026

Shout broadcasts & the new credits store.

NEW IMPROVED

You can now spend credits to /shout a message to everyone currently online. We've also rebuilt the store with cleaner pricing, restored purchases, and theme unlocks.

  • NEWShout broadcast — /shout your message sends to every online user. Costs 12 credits.
  • NEWThemes store with blue_terminal and red_alert unlocks.
  • IMPROVEDStore UI: clearer pricing, instant unlock animation, "restore purchases" works from any tab.
  • IMPROVEDCredit balance now visible in the top bar across all screens.
v1.1.2 Apr 14, 2026

Match flow polish & report fixes.

FIXED IMPROVED
  • FIXEDProfile preview occasionally showed the wrong avatar after a skip. Cache invalidation is correct now.
  • FIXED/report would silently fail if the session ended mid-tap. Reports now queue and submit on next connection.
  • FIXEDConnecting… spinner could stick after backgrounding. Added a 30s timeout with retry.
  • IMPROVEDFaster pairing for users with 4 or more interests selected.
  • IMPROVEDSmoother keyboard-show animation; input bar no longer jumps on first focus.

Themes, ranks, and a real profile.

NEW

The biggest update since launch. Six terminal themes, a 16-tier rank system, and an editable profile with username, bio, and avatar.

  • NEW16-tier rank system — climb from NEW to LEGEND based on session grades.
  • NEWThemes: classic_green, amber, white_phosphor, matrix_dark available at launch. Two more unlockable.
  • NEWProfile tab with username, bio, avatar picker, interest list, and identity card.
  • NEWDashboard with conversation count, average session length, and time online.
  • IMPROVEDInterest picker reorganized into Culture & Entertainment, Tech & Digital, Lifestyle, Sport & Outdoors.
