2026-03-12 fix

fixed reconnect issue during session resume

if your connection dropped mid-conversation, the app would sometimes fail to reconnect silently. this is now fixed — reconnect happens automatically within 3 seconds, and the session resumes cleanly.

2026-03-10 feature

added /dashboard command and credit display

type /dashboard to open your personal control panel. shows your current credit balance, rank, session stats, and quick access to themes and settings — all in the same terminal interface.

2026-03-08 improvement

improved command picker behavior after selection

after selecting a command from the picker, the input field now clears instantly and focus returns to the text area. no more extra tap needed — just pick a command and keep going.
