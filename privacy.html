Privacy Policy

// Last updated: March 7, 2026

stdout.chat is an anonymous chat application. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and what we don't do with it.

// Age requirement

stdout.chat is intended for users 18 years of age or older. By using this app, you confirm that you are at least 18 years old. We do not knowingly allow access to users under 18.

// What we collect

We collect as little as possible:

  • IP address — used for rate limiting, abuse prevention, and automated moderation. Stored only as part of ban records when necessary.
  • Device ID — a randomly generated anonymous identifier stored locally on your device (Keychain). Used for the rating system and block list. It is never linked to your identity.
  • Rating data — your anonymous rating score, number of conversations, and average session duration. Stored on our server linked to your Device ID only.
  • Reports — if you use the /report command, we log the IP address of the reported user and increment a counter. No message content is stored.
  • Connection metadata — timestamps of connections and disconnections for server diagnostics.

// What we do NOT collect

  • We do not store message content. Conversations are ephemeral — they exist only during the session.
  • We do not require registration, name, email, or phone number.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
  • We do not use tracking pixels, advertising networks, or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not link your Device ID to your real identity.

// Automated moderation

stdout.chat uses an automated moderation system. Users who receive multiple reports are automatically restricted based on report thresholds. This system operates without human review of message content — no messages are ever stored or read.

// Rating system

Your rating is calculated based on conversation duration and reports received. It is stored anonymously on our server linked to your Device ID. You can view your rating at any time using the /score command. Your rating is never shared with third parties.

// Block list

When you use the /block command, the blocked user's Device ID is stored locally on your device only. We do not store your block list on our servers.

// Cookies

The app does not use cookies. The website uses no tracking cookies.

// Data retention

  • Connection logs — retained up to 7 days, then deleted.
  • Rating data — retained until you uninstall the app and your Device ID is no longer active.
  • Ban records — retained for the duration of the ban, then deleted.
  • Report counters — retained for moderation purposes.

// Third-party services

  • Vercel — hosts this website. May log standard web access logs.
  • Hetzner — hosts the chat server infrastructure.

// Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
