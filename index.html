// why stdout.chat

🎯

matched by interests

Choose up to 5 interests. We find someone who shares them — so conversations start with something real.

👤

you decide who to talk to

See the other person's profile before connecting — their name, interests, and rating. Accept or move on. Always your choice.

🛡️

safe and moderated

Built-in report and block tools. A community rating system that rewards respectful conversations. Guidelines enforced.

// ranking system

after each chat, you get a grade: S (amazing), A (great), B (decent), C (too short), D (spam).
quality conversations earn more points. streaks give bonus points.
score can go down for bad behavior. you'll be matched with people close to your level.

  • 0 NULL — you barely exist
  • 50 VOID — pure emptiness
  • 120 GHOST — others avoid you
  • 200 SHADOW — barely noticed
  • 300 DRIFTER — wandering
  • 450 WANDERER — searching
  • 650 STRANGER — finding your way
  • 900 KNOWN — people notice you
  • 1200 FAMILIAR — they remember you
  • 1500 TRUSTED — rare
  • 1900 CONNECTED — very rare
  • 2400 VOID WALKER — top tier
  • 3000 PHANTOM — almost mythical
  • 3600 ORACLE — you know things
  • 4200 ARCHITECT — you built this world
  • 5000 LEGEND — top 1%. known by the void.

// commands

  • /next end conversation and find someone new
  • /time see how long you've been connected
  • /clear wipe the conversation from your screen
  • /report report inappropriate behavior
  • /block block this user
  • /quit leave
  • /score your rating, rank and stats
  • /help show all commands
  • /dashboard your credits, missions, streaks, themes store and settings — the terminal control panel
