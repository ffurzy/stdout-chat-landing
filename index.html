// why stdout.chat

⬛

no identity

No account. No username. No number. You're whoever you are in this moment.

🔥

/clear

One command wipes the conversation. It never existed. Move on.

⚡

instant match

Drop in, get connected. No swiping, no waiting rooms. Just strangers.

🖤

terminal ui

Pure monospace. No emojis, no photos, no reactions. Words only.

// ranking system

every conversation shapes your rank. longer chats raise it. reports drop it.
you'll be matched with strangers close to your level.

  • < 400 NULL — you barely exist
  • 400–550 VOID — pure emptiness
  • 550–700 GHOST — others avoid you
  • 700–800 SHADOW — barely noticed
  • 800–880 DRIFTER — wandering
  • 880–950 WANDERER — searching
  • 950–1000 STRANGER — default. you are everyone.
  • 1000–1080 KNOWN — people notice you
  • 1080–1150 FAMILIAR — they remember you
  • 1150–1250 TRUSTED — rare
  • 1250–1350 CONNECTED — very rare
  • 1350–1500 VOID WALKER — top tier
  • 1500–1650 PHANTOM — almost mythical
  • 1650–1800 ORACLE — you know things
  • 1800–1950 ARCHITECT — you built this world
  • > 1950 LEGEND — top 1%. known by the void.

// commands

  • /next disconnect and find a new stranger
  • /time see how long you've been connected
  • /clear wipe the conversation from your screen
  • /report report inappropriate behavior
  • /block block this stranger
  • /quit leave
  • /score your rating, rank and stats
  • /help show all commands
  • /shout broadcast your message to every user online — scrolls across all screens simultaneously
  • /dashboard your credits, shout balance, themes store and settings — the terminal control panel
